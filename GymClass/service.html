FAQs

What are your hours of operation?
We are open Monday through Friday from 5 AM to 10 PM, Saturday from 7 AM to 8 PM, and Sunday from 8 AM to 6 PM.

Do I need to be a member to take classes?
While membership offers the best value, non-members can also join classes by purchasing a day pass.

What types of classes do you offer?
We offer a variety of classes including Zumba, Spin, HIIT, Yoga, Pilates, and Strength Training.

How do I book a class?
Classes can be booked online through our website or mobile app.

What is your cancellation policy?
We require at least 12 hours' notice for class cancellations to avoid a late cancellation fee.

Do you offer personal training?
Yes, we offer personal training sessions that can be tailored to your individual fitness goals.
